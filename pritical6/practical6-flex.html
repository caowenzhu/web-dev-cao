history about baking

Cake

The greatest invention in baking

Cakes are broadly divided into several categories, mainly based on ingredients and mixing techniques. While clear examples of the difference between cakes and breads are easy to find, precise classification has been elusive. Butter cake is made from creamed butter, sugar, eggs and flour.

Bread

God's darling-People's favorite staple food

The so-called bread is made from rye, wheat and other food crops as basic raw materials. It is first ground into powder, then mixed with water, salt, yeast, etc. to make a dough base, which is then heated by baking, roasting, steaming or frying.

Biscuits

Baking elf

Biscuits are a kind of flour-based baked goods. Biscuits come in various shapes and can be filled or decorated. In addition, some baked goods that do not contain flour, such as macaroons and macarons, are also called cookies.

what is baking

The star of parties and afternoon tea

Baking refers to the technology of making baked foods such as bread, cakes, biscuits, pastries, pies, tarts, pizzas, puffs, etc. It is common in Western-style cooking and is usually baked in an oven.
